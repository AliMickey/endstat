Privacy Policy

What data we store

We (endstat) will only store information that is required to perform our website's functionality. This includes the following information:

- Your username, email, hashed password, notification settings, user alerts, password reset requests, your websites, scans of those websites.

Upon requesting account deletion (found in user settings), all the above mentioned data will be permanently deleted.


Where we store data

At the time of writing, endstat is deployed on Digital Ocean's droplet service. All data is stored in the Singapore data center.
This may change in the future.


What we share

We do not share any personal user information with external services.

Notifications

Email
Any notifications sent via email are relayed through SparkPost, view their privacy policy for more information.
Other
Any notification sent via other notification agents are subject to their respective privacy policy.
